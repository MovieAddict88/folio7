══════════════════════════════════════════════
STYLE & DESIGN
══════════════════════════════════════════════
- Colors: Navy Blue (#1C2B4A), White (#FFFFFF), Gold (#E2B714)
- Typography: Poppins or Inter
- Rounded card corners (2xl), subtle shadows
- Micro-animations on hover/click
- Light/Dark mode toggle (auto-detect system)
- Grid/flex layouts for alignment
- Accessibility: Large fonts, ARIA labels, screen reader friendly
- Lazy loading images/videos for performance
- Optional PWA support (installable as app)
